Using Google's MediaPipe Hands model, BrainCoach AI maps 21 precise landmarks on each hand at up to 60fps. Every finger curl, thumb position, and palm orientation is evaluated in real time against the instructor video — giving children instant visual feedback and parents highly granular data.
